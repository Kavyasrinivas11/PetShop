About Us




Founded in 2012, Paws & Wings Pet World began as a small family-run store in Thrissur with a simple dream – to bring joy into homes through healthy, happy pets. What started with just a few cages of budgies and a couple of Labrador puppies has now grown into one of the most trusted pet destinations in Kochi, offering dogs, cats, birds, hamsters, and exotic small pets along with premium food and accessories.

Our History



  • 2012 – Started as a small local shop with birds and basic pet supplies.

  • 2015 – Expanded to include popular dog and cat breeds, partnering with ethical breeders.

  • 2018 – Introduced exotic pets like hamsters, guinea pigs, and rare budgie mutations.

  • 2022 – Launched online pet sales with nationwide delivery for small pets and accessories.
